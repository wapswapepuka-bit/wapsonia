Professional CV Creation

Learn how to create a CV that stands out and increases your chances of getting interviews.

What is a CV?

A Curriculum Vitae (CV) is a document that summarizes your education, skills, experience, and achievements.

Sections of a Professional CV

1. Personal Information

Full name, phone number, email address, and location.

2. Career Objective

A short statement describing your career goals and what you can offer an employer.

3. Education

List your qualifications starting with the most recent.

4. Skills

Include relevant technical and soft skills.

5. Experience

Include internships, attachments, volunteering, and work experience.

6. References

Provide references or state "Available upon request".

Common CV Mistakes

  • Spelling mistakes
  • Using unprofessional email addresses
  • Including unnecessary information
  • Making the CV too long
  • Poor formatting
